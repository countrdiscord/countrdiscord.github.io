About

Countr is a basic counting bot that I created. It is Discord's EASIEST counting bot. Just make a channel #counting and enjoy! Countr is best used above other roles. It changes member's nicknames based on how far they go!

Roles

Countr gives "Roles" based on how far you go. It changes your nickname. Here is the full list!

  • Starter Counter - 10
  • Basic Counter - 25
  • Countr - 100
  • Epical Counter - 250
  • GODLY COUNTER - 500
  • INSANE COUNTER - 1000
